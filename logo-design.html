BLU Media Logo

August 2019

We wanted to exude a passionate, energetic, and optimistic attitude towards uniting people of color - so I used red, orange, and yellow. Red symbolizes passion & energy; yellow symbolizes optimism & happiness; and orange symbolizes community. I used two circles linked together to symbolize the mission of this platform. Circles are good for communicating feelings of unity. And lastly, I went with the Anurati x Helvetica font combo because it communicates that we are both a tech platform and innovative.
